# LIQAA · Brand guidelines

Short version: **clean, restrained, technical**. We are not a B2C app — we don't shout.

## The wordmark

**LIQAA** is set in **Geist** Bold (or a near-equivalent humanist sans). All caps. Slight negative tracking (`letter-spacing: -0.01em`).

The mark is the wordmark itself. We don't use a separate icon or symbol on most surfaces. When we need an icon (favicon, app icon), it's a single capital **L** in a rounded square with our primary blue fill.

## Colour

**Primary**

- LIQAA Blue: `#1d4ed8` (Pantone 2728 C)
- Ink: `#0a0d18`
- Paper: `#ffffff`

**Secondary**

- Slate-500: `#64748b` (body copy on dark)
- Slate-700: `#334155` (body copy on light)
- Emerald: `#10b981` (success / status-up only)
- Amber: `#f59e0b` (warning only)
- Red-600: `#dc2626` (error only — never decoration)

**Forbidden**: gradients in product UI (one exception: the marketing hero). No purples, no greens-as-decoration, no off-brand colour.

## Typography

- **Display + UI**: [Geist](https://vercel.com/font) (free, open). For Arabic + bilingual contexts: [Cairo](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Cairo).
- **Code**: [Geist Mono](https://vercel.com/font/mono).
- **Body sizes**: 14 / 15 / 16 px depending on density.

Font features: enable `cv11`, `ss01`, `ss03` for Geist (gives the slightly taller lowercase that the brand uses).

## The voice

- **Direct.** "Issue a token. Open a room. Connect peers."
- **Respectful of intelligence.** No "imagine if…", no "discover the magic of…".
- **Technical without being exclusionary.** "JWT scoped to one room, 60-minute TTL." Not "secure session ✨".
- **Honest about tradeoffs.** Marketing pages reference our [ADRs](https://github.com/hartemyaakoub/liqaa-architecture). Real engineers can read the receipts.

## Don't do these things

- ❌ Don't add an emoji to the wordmark.
- ❌ Don't say "powered by LIQAA" anywhere we haven't licensed that phrase.
- ❌ Don't put the logo on a busy photographic background. Plain colour or low-saturation.
- ❌ Don't translate "LIQAA" — it stays Latin script everywhere, even in Arabic copy. (Pronunciation guide: lih-KAH.)
- ❌ Don't mix LIQAA with other vendor logos in an "we use these" wall — partners get a dedicated section.
